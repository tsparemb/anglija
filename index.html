Start on the doorstep: Great Hangman, the highest sea cliff in England (318m) and the high point of the whole South West Coast Path — a real morning climb straight from Combe Martin. Then the 1890 water-powered cliff railway at Lynton, the Valley of Rocks (feral goats, cliffs) and the Watersmeet river-gorge walk.
